Our History

Their History

The couple met at one of their previous workplaces, Panera Bread. Rachael helped teach Lucas the ropes and showed him how to become a better coworker. Over time they began to search the weekly schedule to find the times they worked together, getting excited when they could see each other once or twice a week. Quickly, they knew the friendship growing had a special quality to it. The pre- couple began to hang out in the parking lot after our shifts, talking about life. This was about the same time Lucas found his passion for history, and Rachael would let him ramble on about what period of Roman history he was learning that week. As their bond grew, they began seeing each other outside work; Rach taught Luke tennis, as well as how to be more emotionally connected. They grew enamored with each other and connected so easily and deeply. Eight months into their friendship, Luke asked Rach to be his girlfriend at Lydia’s Café, and she said yes!

The Proposal

After three years of love, affection, and hugs, Lucas decided to formally ask Rachael to marry him. While the proposal was a complete surprise to her, they already knew they were to spend their lives together. After Rach and Luke both obtained each other’s parents’ blessings, Luke brainstormed with his parents and the Barker family to come up with a memorable and meaningful proposal. He decided on the Festival of Lights, recruiting Lauren Schmidt to photograph the moment. Lucas proposed to Rachael on December 15th, 2019, to which she said yes. After standing in heavy snowfall, the family and newly engaged couple went to Matt and Katie’s house to celebrate, play games, and have cake!
